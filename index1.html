support us

Since the origin of the Student Chapter, we have organised several Expert Lectures with an aim to bridge the gap between acadamia and the industry. We have initiated Solid Waste Management, Swatch Bharath Abhiyan and Plantation Drives to foster active involvement of students in working for social causes. We have successfully launched five editions of our technical magazine THE AVENUE, which includes some of the most exquisite articles and interviews. We take pride in TEAM ATHARVA that secured 3rd place in the ASCE National Concrete Canoe Competition, held at Krishnapatnam Port, Nellore, organized as a part of the first ever ASCE Student National Conference, India Section, by VIT Vellore in March 2018.
The team requires funds to participate in NCCC and to organise events. Therefore we seek your support to carry out our projects successfully.
